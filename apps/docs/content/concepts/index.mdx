# Concepts

{CONCEPTS_INDEX_LEDE}

The loop in plain terms:

1. **Capture** — users report what felt broken (shake, tap, or widget) with screenshot and context.
2. **Plain read** — severity, category, and cause in English your team already uses.
3. **Connect** — similar reports collapse to one row; hot spots emerge from the graph.
4. **Fix** — optional agent drafts a PR; you merge, edit, or ignore.
5. **Verify** — automated replay checks the fix before you ship.
6. **Remember** — past fixes become rules your editor sees on the next PR.

Every step is observable, auditable, and improves over time — details below when you need the wiring diagram.
