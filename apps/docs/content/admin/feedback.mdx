# Feedback hub

**Route:** `/feedback`

> **Scenario:** You (or a teammate) filed support tickets from the console and
> want one place for active vs shipped feedback with unread replies.

Tabs: **Overview · Active · Shipped · All**. The page shows a status banner,
KPI snapshot strip, and support ticket list. Unread operator replies surface
as chips; open a ticket for the detail modal.

Feature-category tickets with community votes live on the
[Feature board](/admin/feature-board) instead.
