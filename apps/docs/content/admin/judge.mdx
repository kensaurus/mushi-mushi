# Judge dashboard

The Judge page surfaces:

- **Trailing 30-day mean composite** with a 7-day moving average.
- **Per-component breakdown** of judge scores (which prompts struggle).
- **Prompt-A/B status** — every active candidate with its sample size,
  current win/loss vs. the active baseline, and confidence interval.
- **Drift alerts** — when the daily mean drops more than 0.10 from the
  trailing 7-day baseline.
